You are a senior full-stack engineer, AI engineer, and cybersecurity expert.

Build a production-ready SaaS platform called **“Halal Contract Analyzer”** that analyzes legal contracts and flags clauses that may violate Islamic finance principles.

---

## 🎯 OBJECTIVE

The platform must:

* Accept contracts (PDF, DOCX, TXT)
* Extract and segment clauses
* Analyze each clause using AI + rule-based detection
* Flag potentially non-compliant clauses based on:

  * Riba (interest/usury)
  * Gharar (uncertainty)
  * Maysir (gambling/speculation)
  * Prohibited industries (alcohol, gambling, etc.)
* Provide explanations (NOT religious rulings)
* Output a **compliance risk score (0–100)**

IMPORTANT:
The system must NOT declare something “haram” definitively. It must say:
👉 “Potential compliance risk” with explanation.

---

### 2. Clause Segmentation

---

### 3. Clause Analysis Engine

#### A. Rule-Based Detection

Detect keywords like:

* “interest rate”
* “APR”
* “late payment penalty”
* “compound interest”

#### B. AI Analysis

For each clause, call LLM with this structure:

PROMPT TEMPLATE:
"Analyze the following contract clause according to Islamic finance principles. Identify if it contains:

* Riba (interest)
* Gharar (uncertainty)
* Maysir (gambling/speculation)
* Prohibited activities

Return JSON:
{
"risk_level": "low | medium | high",
"category": "...",
"reason": "...",
"confidence": 0-100
}

Clause:
<<<TEXT>>>"
