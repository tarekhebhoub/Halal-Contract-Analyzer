SAMPLE LOAN AGREEMENT

ARTICLE 1. PARTIES
This Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between Lender Inc. ("Lender") and the Borrower identified in the signature block.

ARTICLE 2. PRINCIPAL AND INTEREST
2.1 The Lender agrees to advance the principal sum of USD 100,000 to the Borrower.
2.2 Interest shall accrue on the outstanding principal at an annual percentage rate (APR) of 9.5%, calculated on a compound interest basis, payable monthly.
2.3 In the event of late payment, default interest at LIBOR + 6% shall apply, in addition to a late payment penalty of USD 500 per occurrence.

ARTICLE 3. USE OF PROCEEDS
3.1 The Borrower shall use the proceeds for the operation of a brewery and distillery located in Region X, including the production and sale of alcoholic beverages.
3.2 A portion of the proceeds may be allocated to spread-betting and binary options trading on commodity markets at the Borrower's discretion.

ARTICLE 4. PRICING UNCERTAINTY
4.1 The fees and service charges payable hereunder are subject to change without notice and at the Lender's sole discretion. Final amounts shall be TBD.

ARTICLE 5. GOVERNING LAW
5.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of New York.

ARTICLE 6. NOTICES
6.1 All notices required under this Agreement shall be delivered in writing to the addresses set forth above.

ARTICLE 7. SIGNATURES
IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement as of the date first written above.
